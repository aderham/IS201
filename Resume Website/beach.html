Surfing in Santa Cruz

Experience the thrill of surfing in the beautiful waves of Santa Cruz and exploring what the town has to offer!

Visit the beautiful beaches of Santa Cruz and enjoy the sun, sand, and surf!

Things to Do at Seabright Beach:

Seabright Beach offers sand for miles, a lighthouse, and a view of the Santa Cruz Harbor. Here are some activities you can enjoy at Seabright Beach:

  • Building sandcastles
  • Beach volleyball
  • Surfing lessons

Things to Do at Cowells Beach:

Cowell Beach is known for its scenic beauty and proximity to the Santa Cruz Boardwalk. Here are some activities you can enjoy at Cowell Beach:

  • Beachcombing
  • Strolling on the wharf
  • Watching surfers

Things to Do at Capitola Beach:

Capitola Beach is a charming seaside village with a vibrant atmosphere. Here are some activities you can enjoy at Capitola Beach:

  • Shopping in Capitola Village
  • Beach picnics
  • Paddleboarding in the bay
